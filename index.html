Background Story

I first discovered the art of web development in early 2017. I've always had a subtle interest for computer development, whether it was game or web development, I've always found it interesting. So, when I stumbled upon some free courses on FreeCodeCamp and discovered that I could learn this on my own for free, I dove in full fledged. I started with their HTML course, and slowly began learning CSS. After making a few ideas and mockups, I shortly began to become a little more confident with HTML & CSS. I would then for the next few years, on and off code ideas and mockups for friend projects (i.e. static shopping pages), personal bio pages, etc. I am now ready to tackle everything else this skill has to provide.
